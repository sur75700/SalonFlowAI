SalonFlowAI is available in simple business-focused packages designed to match different levels of operational complexity.

- Services
- Basic setup

## Professional

Best for salons that want stronger visibility and reporting.

Includes:
- Everything in Starter
- Insights
- PDF reports
- Workspace utilities

## Premium

Best for salons that want an advanced operational setup.

Includes:
- Everything in Professional
- Priority support
- Premium business setup direction

- Advanced reporting direction
