# SalonFlowAI Sales Map Summary

## Map

Lead -> Outreach -> Reply -> Qualification -> Demo -> Offer -> Close -> Onboard

## Key Movement

- not every lead becomes warm
- not every warm lead becomes demo
- not every demo becomes close

The system wins by:
- enough lead intake
- disciplined movement
- clean follow-up
- strong demo quality
- smart closing

## Simplified Focus

1. Find better leads
2. Send better outreach
3. Follow up faster
4. Demo stronger
5. Close cleaner\n
