# SalonFlowAI Follow-Up Scheduler

## Recommended Timing

### After first outreach
Follow up in 2 to 3 days.

### After second outreach
Follow up in 3 to 5 days.

### After demo invitation
Follow up in 2 days if there is no reply.

### After demo completed
Follow up in 1 to 2 days with a summary and next step.

### After offer sent
Follow up in 2 to 4 days.

## Follow-Up Logic

- Warm lead -> shorter follow-up window
- Cold lead -> slower follow-up window
- High-value lead -> more attention and faster response

## Best Practice

Every lead should always have:
- last contact date
- next follow-up date
- current status
- next action\n
