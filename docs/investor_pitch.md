# SalonFlowAI Investor Pitch

## Product
SalonFlowAI is an admin operating system for salon businesses. It brings bookings, client records, service catalog control, business insights, and PDF reporting into one unified workspace.

## Problem
Many salons still run on scattered tools, manual notes, chat-based coordination, and weak reporting. This creates lost bookings, weak visibility, poor client tracking, and low operational control.

## Solution
SalonFlowAI provides:
- Dashboard overview for daily operating visibility
- Booking control for scheduling and status management
- Client registry for organized customer records
- Service catalog management for pricing, duration, and active state
- Insights for revenue and booking intelligence
- PDF export flow for daily summary reporting
- Workspace shortcuts for fast operator navigation

## Why It Matters
SalonFlowAI turns salon operations into a controlled system instead of a manual process. It reduces chaos, improves visibility, and creates a stronger business foundation for scaling.
