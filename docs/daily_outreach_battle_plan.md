# SalonFlowAI Daily Outreach Battle Plan

## Daily Target

Every working day aim for:
- 10 new outreach messages
- 5 follow-ups
- 2 warm conversations
- 1 demo push

## Daily Sequence

### Block 1 — Lead Discovery
- find 5 new leads
- rank them quickly
- record them in the lead list

### Block 2 — First Outreach
- send 5 first messages
- use the correct channel-specific template
- personalize the first line

### Block 3 — Follow-Up
- send 5 follow-ups to older leads
- move warm replies toward demo

### Block 4 — CRM Update
- update status
- add next follow-up date
- record notes and objections

### Block 5 — Demo Push
- ask at least 1 qualified lead for a short demo

## Core Rule

Do not finish the day without:
- new leads
- new outreach
- updated statuses
- at least one next-step action\n
