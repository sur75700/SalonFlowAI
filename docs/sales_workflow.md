# SalonFlowAI Sales Workflow

## Step 1 — Find Leads
Build a lead list of salons, beauty studios, and appointment-based businesses.

## Step 2 — First Outreach
Send the first message with a short and clear value proposition.

## Step 3 — Qualify
Check whether the business has real admin pain and operational fit.

## Step 4 — Invite To Demo
Move the lead toward a short walkthrough.

## Step 5 — Show Demo
Focus on business workflow, not only on UI.

## Step 6 — Match The Right Package
Recommend Starter, Professional, or Premium based on fit.

## Step 7 — Handle Objections
Answer calmly and move toward clarity.

## Step 8 — Close
Ask for the next business step directly and professionally.

## Step 9 — Onboard
Prepare the setup process and help the client start correctly.

## Rule Of The System

Every lead should have:
- a status
- a next action
- a next follow-up date
- a business note\n
