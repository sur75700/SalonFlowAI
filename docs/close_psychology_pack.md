# SalonFlowAI Close Psychology Pack

## Main Principle

People rarely buy only features.
They buy confidence in the result.

## What Helps Closing

- clarity
- confidence
- relevance
- calm recommendation
- visible business fit
- low confusion

## What Blocks Closing

- unclear value
- too much complexity
- weak recommendation
- no next step
- too much hesitation from the seller

## Close Structure

1. confirm pain
2. confirm fit
3. recommend package
4. define next step
5. ask clearly

## Useful Closing Lines

- Based on your workflow, I believe this is a strong fit.
- The next step would be to move into the setup option that matches your business best.
- If you want, we can move forward with the package that fits your current level of operational need.\n
