# SalonFlowAI Sales To Cash Workflow

## Step 1 — Lead
A potential client is identified.

## Step 2 — Outreach
The first contact is made.

## Step 3 — Reply
The lead responds or moves into follow-up.

## Step 4 — Qualification
The lead's pain and fit are understood.

## Step 5 — Demo
The product is shown with business relevance.

## Step 6 — Offer
The right package is recommended.

## Step 7 — Close
The lead agrees to move forward.

## Step 8 — Onboarding
The client is set up and supported.

## Step 9 — Cash Realization
Revenue becomes real through setup fee, monthly payment, or both.

## Main Rule

Do not stop at demo.
Push the workflow toward actual cash.\n
