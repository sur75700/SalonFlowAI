# SalonFlowAI Market Attack Notes

## Attack Style

Focused, direct, professional, repeatable.

## What Wins

- good lead selection
- clean messaging
- fast follow-up
- strong demo
- simple closing

## What Loses

- random outreach
- weak positioning
- no tracking
- too much complexity
- slow follow-up

## Attack Rule

Every day should produce one of these:
- new leads
- new outreach
- new replies
- new demos
- new closing progress

## Business Mindset

Treat acquisition like a system, not like luck.\n
