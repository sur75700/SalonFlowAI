# Revenue Priority Rules

## Priority 1
Warm leads with real fit.

## Priority 2
Completed demos without clear next step.

## Priority 3
Qualified leads who have not yet seen a demo.

## Priority 4
Fresh high-score leads.

## Priority 5
Cold leads for lower-energy follow-up.

## Main Rule

Always give the strongest time and attention to:
- the closest money
- the strongest fit
- the warmest opportunity\n
