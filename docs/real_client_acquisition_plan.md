# SalonFlowAI Real Client Acquisition Plan

## Mission

The goal is to move from product readiness to real business acquisition.

## Core Objective

Get the first real salon clients through a focused outreach and demo process.

## Main Strategy

- identify strong-fit salon businesses
- contact them directly
- qualify pain points
- move them to demo
- recommend the right package
- close the first paying customers

## First Target Market

- small salons
- medium salons
- beauty studios
- barbershops
- appointment-based beauty businesses

## Why This Market

These businesses often have:
- real booking needs
- client record needs
- service catalog complexity
- reporting gaps
- operational chaos

## Main Acquisition Channels

- Instagram business pages
- Facebook business pages
- WhatsApp contacts
- Telegram business contacts
- Google Maps business search
- local business directories
- direct referrals

## Core Principle

Do not wait for inbound traffic first.
Start with direct client acquisition and controlled outreach.\n
