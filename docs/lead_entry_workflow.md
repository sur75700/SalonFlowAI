# SalonFlowAI Lead Entry Workflow

## Step 1 — Find The Lead

Collect the business from:
- Instagram
- Facebook
- Google Maps
- local directories
- referrals

## Step 2 — Record The Basics

Always capture:
- business name
- contact person if visible
- city
- contact channels
- business type
- lead source

## Step 3 — Add Qualification Notes

Write:
- appointment-heavy or not
- likely service complexity
- signs of admin pain
- why they are a fit

## Step 4 — Set Initial Status

Use:
- New
or
- Contacted

## Step 5 — Set Next Action

Examples:
- send first Instagram DM
- send email
- follow up in 3 days
- propose demo

## Main Rule

A lead should never exist without:
- status
- note
- next action
- next date\n
