# Money Pull 15 — Week 1 Execution Pack

## Week 1 Mission

Turn early outreach into visible sales momentum.

## Day Structure

### Day 1
- build lead base
- send first messages

### Day 2
- add more leads
- continue first outreach

### Day 3
- push warm leads
- begin follow-up cycle

### Day 4
- follow up on silent leads
- move interested leads toward demo

### Day 5
- propose demos
- identify strongest lead

### Day 6
- run demo or prepare strongest demo route

### Day 7
- review week
- rank next opportunities
- plan next week

## Week 1 Output Goal

- 15 to 20 leads
- 10+ first messages
- 5+ follow-ups
- at least 2 warm leads
- at least 1 serious demo path\n
