# SalonFlowAI Lead Qualification Notes

## Strong Lead Signals

- They already take appointments regularly
- They have multiple services
- They manage many client records
- They mention operational confusion
- They mention admin overload
- They want stronger reporting
- They want a more professional system

## Questions To Understand Fit

- How do you currently manage bookings?
- How do you track client records?
- Do you manage service pricing from one place?
- Do you currently have reporting visibility?
- Are you using multiple tools today?
- Would a single admin workspace help your workflow?

## Best Early Customers

- Small and medium salons
- Beauty studios
- Appointment-heavy businesses
- Owners who want more control and cleaner systems

## Red Flags

- No interest in improving workflow
- No need for bookings/client organization
- Only casual hobby use
- No operational pain at all

## Closing Note

Do not push too early.                                                                                                       The goal is to identify real pain, show fit, and move the lead toward a short demo.\n
