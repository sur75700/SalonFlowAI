## Objective

Turn early satisfied clients into proof and growth assets.

## Referral Opportunity

Once a client is clearly happy, ask whether they know another salon or beauty business that may need a cleaner system.

## Testimonial Opportunity

Ask after visible value has been delivered, not too early.

If SalonFlowAI is helping your workflow, I would appreciate a short testimonial about how it supports your salon operations.

## Referral Prompt

If you know another salon owner who may benefit from cleaner booking, client, service, and reporting control, I would be happy to show them a short demo as well.
