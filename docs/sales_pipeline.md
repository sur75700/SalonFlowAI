# SalonFlowAI Sales Pipeline

## Stage 1 — New Lead
The lead is identified but not yet contacted.

## Stage 2 — Contacted
Initial message has been sent.

## Stage 3 — Replied
The lead answered and showed some level of engagement.

## Stage 4 — Qualified
The lead appears to have a real operational need and fits the product.

## Stage 5 — Demo Proposed
A short product walkthrough has been offered.

## Stage 6 — Demo Booked
A time has been agreed for a demo.

## Stage 7 — Demo Completed
The product has been shown and fit has been discussed.

## Stage 8 — Offer Sent
Pricing or package discussion has started.

## Stage 9 — Negotiation
The lead is comparing options or asking for details.

## Stage 10 — Closed Won
The client accepted and moved forward.

## Stage 11 — Closed Lost
The opportunity did not move forward.

## Key Rule

Always move the lead to the next clear action.
Do not let leads sit without a defined next step.\n
