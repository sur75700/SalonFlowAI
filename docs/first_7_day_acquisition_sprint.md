# SalonFlowAI First 7-Day Acquisition Sprint

## Day 1
- build 10 leads
- send 5 outreach messages

## Day 2
- build 10 more leads
- send 5 more outreach messages
- follow up with day 1 contacts if relevant

## Day 3
- build final 10 leads
- send 10 outreach messages
- organize tracker

## Day 4
- send first follow-ups
- identify warm replies
- propose demos

## Day 5
- continue follow-ups
- book first demos
- prepare package recommendations

## Day 6
- run demos
- log objections
- send post-demo summary

## Day 7
- follow up on demos
- move strongest leads toward offer and close

## Sprint Goal

By the end of 7 days:
- 30 leads found
- 20+ messages sent
- multiple follow-ups sent
- at least 2 demo conversations attempted
- at least 1 strong opportunity created\n
