# Lead Hunt Checklist

## Before Adding A Lead

Check:
- active business presence
- visible appointment relevance
- reachable contact
- service variety
- owner/business seriousness
- signs of workflow pain

## What To Capture

- business name
- segment
- city/area
- Instagram
- Facebook
- WhatsApp / phone
- email
- service complexity
- likely pain point
- lead score
- strongest outreach channel

## Ranking Rule

The lead should be easy to classify as:
- top priority
- strong fit
- test fit\n
