# SalonFlowAI Armenia Market Strike Plan

## Objective

Launch a focused client acquisition push in the Armenian market for salon and beauty businesses.

## Main Goal

Get the first real Armenian salon clients through direct outreach, demo booking, and business-fit positioning.

## Core Strategy

- target appointment-based beauty businesses
- focus on owner pain and admin control
- use Armenian-friendly messaging
- move warm leads to demo quickly
- close the first local clients

## Why Armenia First

- easier local understanding
- easier language adaptation
- stronger cultural positioning
- easier direct contact and follow-up
- better trust building in early sales

## Local Focus Areas

- Yerevan
- nearby active beauty districts
- growing studio-based businesses
- appointment-driven salons and beauty studios

## Main Rule

Start concentrated. Win locally first. Expand after proof.\n
