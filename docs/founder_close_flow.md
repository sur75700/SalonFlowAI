# Founder Close Flow

## Step 1 — Understand
Know the business pain before pitching too much.

## Step 2 — Show
Demonstrate the workflow with business relevance.

## Step 3 — Confirm
Ask whether the workflow matches their real needs.

## Step 4 — Recommend
Do not ask the lead to solve your pricing logic.
Recommend the right package.

## Step 5 — Ask
Use a clear next-step question.

## Strong Founder Close Line

Based on what you shared, I believe SalonFlowAI is a strong fit for your workflow. The next step would be to move forward with the package that best matches your operational needs.

## Main Rule

A close should feel natural, not forced.\n
