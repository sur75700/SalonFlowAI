# SalonFlowAI 30-Lead Hunt System

## Goal

Build a focused list of 30 strong-fit leads.

## Daily Structure

### Day 1
Find 10 salon leads.

### Day 2
Find 10 more leads.

### Day 3
Find final 10 leads.

## Where To Search

- Instagram search by salon keywords
- Facebook business pages
- Google Maps
- local directories
- salon hashtags
- city-based beauty business listings

## What To Collect

- business name
- city
- Instagram
- Facebook
- WhatsApp / phone
- email
- service type
- visible booking method
- quality of current online presence
- possible pain points

## Priority Signals

Strong lead if:
- they actively post
- they offer multiple services
- they clearly take appointments
- they look operationally busy
- they do not appear to have a strong management system

## Output Target

At the end of the hunt, have:
- 30 identified businesses
- 10 high-priority leads
- 10 medium-priority leads
- 10 backup leads\n
