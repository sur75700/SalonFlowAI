# SalonFlowAI Outreach To Close Workflow

## Stage 1 — Lead Found
A salon business is identified and logged.

## Stage 2 — Lead Ranked
The lead receives a fit score and channel choice.

## Stage 3 — First Outreach
Initial message is sent using the correct script and personalized opener.

## Stage 4 — Follow-Up
If no response, follow-up sequence is used.

## Stage 5 — Warm Reply
If the lead responds, qualification begins.

## Stage 6 — Qualification
Understand:
- booking process
- client record pain
- service complexity
- reporting need
- admin chaos level

## Stage 7 — Demo Push
Offer a short demo focused on business workflow.

## Stage 8 — Demo
Show:
- Dashboard
- Bookings
- Clients
- Services
- Insights
- PDF Reports
- Workspace

## Stage 9 — Package Fit
Recommend:
- Starter
- Professional
- Premium

## Stage 10 — Close
Move to:
- next meeting
- setup discussion
- onboarding
- agreement

## Stage 11 — Onboard
Turn the lead into an active client relationship.\n
