# First Close Route

## Goal

Move the strongest first lead from outreach to demo to close.

## Step 1 — Warm Reply
Acknowledge interest and move quickly.

## Step 2 — Short Explanation
Explain SalonFlowAI as a salon operations control system.

## Step 3 — Demo Push
Offer a short walkthrough focused on:
- bookings
- clients
- services
- insights
- reporting

## Step 4 — Fit Confirmation
Ask whether the workflow matches the business.

## Step 5 — Package Recommendation
Recommend Starter, Professional, or Premium.

## Step 6 — Clear Next Step
Move toward:
- setup discussion
- onboarding discussion
- first agreement

## Main Rule

Do not delay after a strong demo.\n
