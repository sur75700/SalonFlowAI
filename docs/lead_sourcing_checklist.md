# SalonFlowAI Lead Sourcing Checklist

## Before Adding A Lead

Check:
- does the business clearly serve clients by appointment?
- do they have multiple services?
- do they look active?
- is there a reachable contact channel?
- do they seem to have operational complexity?

## Good Signs

- strong Instagram activity
- service menu visible
- booking link or booking mention
- customer traffic
- multiple specialists
- quality visuals but weak admin structure

## Weak Signs

- inactive page
- no clear business activity
- no contact path
- hobby-style page
- no evidence of appointments

## Always Record

- business name
- contact channel
- city
- business type
- lead source
- why they are a fit\n
