# SalonFlowAI Conversion Playbook

## Stage 1 — Attention

Use a short message with clear business value.

## Stage 2 — Interest

Explain that SalonFlowAI helps manage bookings, clients, services, insights, and reports from one system.

## Stage 3 — Qualification

Check if the lead has:
- admin pain
- booking needs
- client record needs
- reporting gaps

## Stage 4 — Demo

Show only the most relevant workflow:
- dashboard
- bookings
- clients
- services
- insights
- reports

## Stage 5 — Fit

Match the right package:
- Starter
- Professional
- Premium

## Stage 6 — Close

Ask for the next business step directly.

## Core Conversion Rule

Do not overload the lead with too much detail too early.
Move them one step at a time.\n
