# SalonFlowAI Revenue Roadmap

## Phase 1 — First Paying Client

Main goal:
- close the first client
- prove business fit
- gain confidence
- validate onboarding flow

## Phase 2 — First 3 Clients

Main goal:
- repeat the close process
- improve messaging
- collect feedback
- sharpen demo flow

## Phase 3 — First 5 Clients

Main goal:
- create stable early revenue
- identify strongest package
- identify strongest segment
- improve retention and support process

## Phase 4 — First 10 Clients

Main goal:
- turn the sales motion into a repeatable system
- strengthen onboarding
- build client proof and testimonials
- improve business predictability

## Revenue Principle

Do not obsess over scale first.
Obsess over repeatable wins first.\n
