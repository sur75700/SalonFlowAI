# SalonFlowAI Pricing Conversation Flow

## When To Talk Pricing

Do not start with pricing before value is understood.

## Best Timing

Pricing is strongest after:
- need is identified
- workflow fit is visible
- demo is shown
- objections are understood

## Pricing Flow

### Step 1
Frame the packages simply:
- Starter
- Professional
- Premium

### Step 2
Explain that the right setup depends on workflow needs and support level.

### Step 3
Recommend the package instead of forcing the lead to guess.

### Step 4
Keep the conversation tied to business fit, not just numbers.

## Pricing Language

The best package depends on how much operational visibility, reporting, and setup depth you need. Based on your workflow, I would recommend starting with the option that fits your business complexity.

## Rule

Do not sound uncertain.
Recommend clearly.\n
