# SalonFlowAI First Client Strategy

## Main Goal

The first client matters more than perfect scale.

## What The First Client Should Give You

- proof of business value
- practical feedback
- stronger sales confidence
- testimonial potential
- clearer onboarding process

## Ideal First Client Profile

- appointment-based
- owner accessible
- operationally active
- open to improvement
- not overly complex

## Offer Strategy

Use a simple, confidence-building offer:
- clear package
- simple explanation
- low confusion
- strong onboarding support

## Closing Strategy

Focus on:
- business fit
- workflow clarity
- confidence
- speed to decision

## Main Rule

Do not chase only the biggest business first.
Win the best-fit business first.\n
