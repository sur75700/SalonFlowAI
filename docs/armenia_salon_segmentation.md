# Armenia Salon Segmentation

## Primary Segments

### Hair Salons
Strong fit because of:
- recurring bookings
- multiple services
- client tracking needs
- busy daily flow

### Beauty Studios
Strong fit because of:
- client record importance
- service variety
- appointment dependence
- owner-led operations

### Nail Studios
Good fit because of:
- repeat clients
- booking dependency
- workflow organization needs

### Brow / Lash Studios
Good fit because of:
- recurring appointments
- customer retention focus
- simple but high-value workflow structure

### Barbershops
Useful fit because of:
- appointment management
- service control
- operational speed and consistency

## Best First Buyers

- active salon owner
- visible appointment flow
- more than one service
- signs of admin overload
- interest in stronger business control\n
