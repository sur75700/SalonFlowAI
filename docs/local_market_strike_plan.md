# SalonFlowAI Local Market Strike Plan

## Objective

Attack one local market in a focused and professional way.

## Step 1 — Choose A Zone

Pick one city or one concentrated area first.

## Step 2 — Build The List

Find salon businesses in that local area.

## Step 3 — Rank Them

Rank by:
- activity
- professionalism
- likely appointment volume
- visible admin need

## Step 4 — Outreach In Waves

### Wave 1
Top 10 strongest businesses

### Wave 2
Next 10 businesses

### Wave 3
Last 10 backup businesses

## Step 5 — Follow-Up

Do not send one message only.
Use a professional follow-up sequence.

## Step 6 — Demo Concentration

Try to stack demos in the same week.

## Why This Works

A concentrated local push is easier to manage than trying to sell everywhere at once.\n
