# Cash Discipline Notes

## Core Principle

Revenue must be treated seriously from the beginning.

## What To Protect

- clarity of offer
- clean package explanation
- clean close language
- confidence in asking for the next step
- discipline in follow-up

## Early Cash Rule

Even small early revenue matters because it proves:
- someone values the system
- the market can pay
- the workflow can convert
- the business can move beyond theory

## Founder Reminder

Do not hide from asking for the close.
If the fit is real, the next step should be asked clearly.\n
