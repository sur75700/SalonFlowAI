# Armenia Lead Scoring Grid

## Score 5 — Top Priority
- active business
- clear appointment flow
- multiple services
- visible operational complexity
- reachable contact
- strong fit for demo

## Score 4 — Strong Fit
- active salon
- good contact path
- visible booking relevance
- likely admin pain

## Score 3 — Good Fit
- possible admin need
- enough service structure
- worth outreach

## Score 2 — Weak / Moderate Fit
- limited visibility
- unclear appointment flow
- still possible to test

## Score 1 — Low Priority
- weak activity
- unclear fit
- low urgency

## Rule

Spend more energy on 4 and 5 score leads first.\n
