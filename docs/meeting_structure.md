# SalonFlowAI Meeting Structure

## Recommended Meeting Flow

### Part 1 — Context
Understand the business and current admin situation.

### Part 2 — Pain
Identify the real friction:
- booking issues
- client tracking
- service management
- reporting visibility
- owner control

### Part 3 — Demo
Show only the relevant workflow.

### Part 4 — Fit
Check whether the system matches their actual operations.

### Part 5 — Offer
Recommend the right package.

### Part 6 — Next Step
Do not end without a clear next action.

## Meeting Goal

A meeting is successful if it creates:
- clarity
- fit understanding
- a next action\n
