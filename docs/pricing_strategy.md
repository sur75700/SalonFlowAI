# SalonFlowAI Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

The pricing should feel professional, simple, and easy to understand. The first goal is to remove friction and close early customers faster.

## Pricing Model

### Starter
For salons that want the core operating foundation.

Includes:
- Dashboard
- Bookings
- Clients
- Services
- Basic setup

Suggested structure:
- Setup fee: custom
- Monthly fee: lower entry level

### Professional
For salons that want stronger operational visibility.

Includes:
- Everything in Starter
- Insights
- PDF reports
- Workspace utilities
- Onboarding support

Suggested structure:
- Setup fee: medium
- Monthly fee: standard business tier

### Premium
For salons that want a more advanced business setup.

Includes:
- Everything in Professional
- Priority support
- Custom branding direction
- Multi-location readiness
- Custom reporting direction

Suggested structure:
- Setup fee: premium
- Monthly fee: premium

## Recommended Sales Strategy

At the beginning, sell with:
- Setup fee + monthly subscription
or
- Trial onboarding + monthly subscription

## Closing Guidance

Do not start with too much pricing complexity.
Start with:
- Starter
- Professional
- Premium

Then adapt the exact numbers to:
- client size
- salon complexity
- onboarding effort
- support expectations

## Suggested Sales Framing

Starter -> strong base
Professional -> best value
Premium -> advanced growth package\n
