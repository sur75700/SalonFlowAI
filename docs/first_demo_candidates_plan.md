# First Demo Candidates Plan

## Objective

Identify the first 2 to 3 leads most likely to accept a demo.

## Best Signals

- fast reply
- clear pain
- asks for more info
- already curious
- operational language in replies

## What To Do

- answer quickly
- keep the explanation short
- move to demo invite fast
- offer a simple short walkthrough

## Demo Candidate Rule

Warm interest should be moved toward demo quickly, not left idle.\n
