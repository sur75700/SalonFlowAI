# SalonFlowAI Closing Checklist

## Before Closing

- The lead understands the product
- The lead sees the operational value
- The lead has seen the relevant screens
- The right package is identified
- Questions have been answered
- Objections have been handled
- The next step is clear

## Closing Questions

- Does this workflow fit the way your salon operates?
- Which package feels closest to your current needs?
- Would you like to start with a short onboarding setup?
- Should we move to the next step and prepare your setup?

## After Agreement

- Confirm package
- Confirm onboarding flow
- Confirm timeline
- Confirm support expectations
- Confirm payment process
- Confirm launch/start date\n
