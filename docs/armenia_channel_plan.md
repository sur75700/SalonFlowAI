# Armenia Channel Plan

## Main Outreach Channels

### Instagram
Best for:
- active beauty brands
- salons with visible social presence
- direct page messaging

### Facebook
Best for:
- established salon pages
- older business owners
- longer business-style messages

### WhatsApp / Phone
Best for:
- warm follow-up
- direct local communication
- demo scheduling

### Telegram
Useful when:
- business contact prefers messaging apps
- direct communication is easier there

### Google Maps / Local Listings
Best for:
- finding businesses
- collecting names, numbers, locations
- building the lead base

## Channel Sequence

1. find business
2. log contact data
3. send first message on strongest channel
4. follow up on second channel if needed
5. move warm lead to demo\n
